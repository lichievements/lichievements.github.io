Hints & move lists for the achievements that are hard to stumble into.

The Opening Collections ask you to play a whole set of specific openings, so they rarely unlock by accident. Below is exactly what each one needs. Play the listed moves in any of your games and the corresponding member ticks off — except for Encyclopedia, the colour doesn't matter, either side counts, and you only need the opening to appear once each.
